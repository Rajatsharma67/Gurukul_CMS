# Gurukul_CMS
All the colleges basically have websites through which they provide  information about the college, course offered and most important  for them and students of the colleges to provides study material,  student details, syllabus the proposed Project handles the student  list, upload or download study material (notes) , syllabus through a  website of the college.  The aim is to automate its existing manual system By the help of computerized equipment’s and full – fledged website, Fulfilling their requirements, so that their valuable data or  information Can be stored for a longer period with easy accessing  and manipulation of the same.  Basically the Project describes how to manage the student list, Provide them study material (notes), syllabus. For easy and convenient processing we need to develop a website, Which works as per our requirements.
